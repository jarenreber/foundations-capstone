Showing you the best bike trails of Southern Utah

Gila Outdoors, also known as Gila Out, is a site for finding the best trails and rides in the Southern Utah for mountain biking. We are passionate about giving our users the best and most up to date information possible. Feel free to reach out to us to add more trails and do more exploring.

With Gila outdoors you're sure to have the best time possible.

What is a Gila?

Gila is short for a Gila monster, a native lizard to the southwestern United States. We chose Gila as our name mostly because it sounds awesome, and even more, the lizard looks awesome!
